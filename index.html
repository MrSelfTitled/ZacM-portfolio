About Me

Hey! I’m Zac Main, I am a Creative and dedicated filmmaker with six years of experience, specializing in music video production and personal projects. I have collaborated with Self~Titled, The Ivy Walkers, and Orphix to bring artist visions to life through compelling visuals and storytelling. My work demonstrates a strong aestheticsense and a commitment to pushing creative boundaries, whether workingin collaboration or on solo projects. Skilled in directing, cinematography, and post-production, I am passionate about creating impactful, memorable media. I’m eager to bring my experience and collaborative spirit to new projects and contribute to innovative storytelling in the film industry.
